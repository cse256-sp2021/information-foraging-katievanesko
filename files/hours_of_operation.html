Program Hours

Schedule Options:

Children have the opportunity to attend WUNS for mornings only, afternoons only, or the full day option.


Morning: 9:00 am - 11:45 am
Afternoon: 12:30 pm - 3:15 pm
Full day: 9:00 am - 3:15 pm

Classes meet Monday- Friday

The school calendar runs from early September to mid-May.
